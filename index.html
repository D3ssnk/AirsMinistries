MISSION OBJECTIVE

Airs Ministries is a church that believes in Jesus, a church that loves God and its people.

Le mot AIRS, comme sigle, signifie: Action Internationale pour la Réveil Spirituel (International Action for Spiritual Revival). Au Royaume Uni, AIRS Ministries a été fondée en Juillet 1992, sous les auspices du Rev. Mabs Nkumu, le fondateur, le leader, et pére spirituel du Ministére. Vision et orientation de AIRS: Le réveil spiritual permettant á l'homme nouveau de découvrir sa mission céleste et sa mission terrestre á devenir ainsi leader de sa vie, afin d'influencer sa génération. The word AIRS, is an acronym, that stands for: International Action for Spiritual Revival. In the UK, AIRS Ministries was founded in July 1992 under the auspices of Rev. Mabs Nkumu, the founder, leader, and spiritual father of the Ministry. AIRS Vision and Direction: The spiritual awakening allows the new person to discover his celestial mission and his earthly mission to thus become a leader of his/her life, in order to influence their generation.
